The 2026 Regulatory Lifecycle

Precision Documentation & Inter-Agency Liaison

P

Pioneer Status (PSI)

01

NIPC Gazette Alignment

We verify your company’s activity against the **List of Pioneer Industries**. We handle the formal NIPC application and the non-refundable processing fee of ₦200,000+.

02

Technical Case Defense

Preparation of the **Project Impact Assessment**. We document your 5-year CAPEX plan, employment projections, and local value addition to secure the "Approval-in-Principle."

03

IID Verification Visit

The **Industrial Inspectorate Division** conducts a physical audit to certify your "Production Day." This is a critical legal milestone—your tax holiday start date is fixed here.

04

FIRS Compliance Integration

Post-issuance, we manage your annual performance reports to the NIPC and ensure the FIRS recognizes your **exempt status** during annual tax filings.

C

Acceptance (CAFA)

01

Asset Inventory & Audit

We analyze all assets > ₦500k. We compile your **Form IID 01**, ensuring every invoice, bill of lading, and customs entry matches the physical asset on-site.

02

Regulatory Inspection Logistics

We coordinate with the **Federal Ministry of Industry, Trade and Investment**. We lead the on-site verification to defend the "Original Cost" of your machinery and infrastructure.

03

Certificate Issuance

Processing and collection of the **Certificate of Acceptance**. This document is the only legal proof allowed by the FIRS for claiming capital allowances on large investments.

04

Tax Liability Shield

The Final Milestone: Your tax payable is significantly reduced by applying the **Capital Allowance** rates against your corporate profit, backed by the CAFA.
